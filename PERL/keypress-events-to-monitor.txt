FOR GENERAL ARROW KEY USAGE:

Up arrow:
	keysym=Up, numeric=65362

Down arrow (Use for shields in future):
	keysym=Down, numeric=65364

Left arrow:
	keysym=Left, numeric=65361

Right arrow:
	keysym=Right, numeric=65363

Space/Fire bar:
	keysym=space, numeric=32

f key for Fire:
	keysym=f, numeric=102

	keysym=s, numeric=115
